# Review Policy

MOSAIC is curated.

Not every submission will be accepted automatically. The goal is to keep the catalog useful, readable, and credible.

## Review criteria

### 1. Validity
We check that:
- the project is real
- the project is publicly accessible
- the project is open source or clearly open
- the project has a meaningful AI component

### 2. Consistency
We check that:
- the entry follows the schema
- taxonomy values are valid
- links and platforms are coherent
- labels are used in a reasonable way

### 3. Accuracy
We check that:
- descriptions are factual
- affiliations are not misleading
- dates and licenses are not obviously wrong
- the Moroccan connection is clear

## Possible outcomes

- **Accepted**
- **Accepted with edits**
- **Needs clarification**
- **Rejected**

## Editorial normalization

Maintainers may make small edits to improve consistency:
- capitalization
- tag cleanup
- taxonomy normalization
- wording cleanup
- affiliation cleanup
